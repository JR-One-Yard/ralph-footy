## DID YOU KNOW?

When a bookie offers $1.34 on a team, they're implying that team wins about 75% of the time. But bookies build in a margin (called the 'overround'), so the TRUE implied probability is closer to 70%. That gap between the raw number and the true number? That's the bookie's cut. This week, the biggest favourite is the Sharks at $1.34.
